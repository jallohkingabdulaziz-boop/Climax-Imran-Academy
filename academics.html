Examination & Assessment Results

At Climax Imran Academy, we are proud of our students’ consistent excellence in national examinations, reflecting the hard work of our learners and dedication of our staff. Our strong results showcase our commitment to academic success and preparing students for future opportunities.

Senior Secondary School (WASSCE) Results

  • 2021: Out of 530 candidates, 410 achieved five credits and above, including English Language and Core Mathematics.
  • 2022: Out of 803 candidates, 646 attained five credits and above, including English Language and Core Mathematics.
  • 2023: Out of 530 candidates, 487 secured five credits and above, including English Language and Core Mathematics.
  • 2024: Out of 431 candidates, 387 achieved five credits and above, including English Language and Core Mathematics.
  • 2025: Awaiting results with confidence for continued success.

Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE) Results

  • 2021: All 52 pupils passed eight subjects, with first position aggregate 16.
  • 2022: All 87 pupils passed, with first position aggregate 15.
  • 2023: All 89 pupils passed, with first position aggregate 12.
  • 2024: All 94 pupils passed with distinction, first position aggregate 11.
  • 2025: Awaiting results, expecting sustained success.

National Primary School Examination (NPSE) Results

  • 2020: All 16 pupils passed with flying colours, first position aggregate 318.
  • 2021: All 35 pupils passed with flying colours, first position aggregate 295.
  • 2022: All 42 pupils passed with flying colours, first position aggregate 305.
  • 2023: All 54 pupils passed with flying colours, first position aggregate 311.
  • 2024: All 63 pupils passed with flying colours, first position aggregate 301.
  • 2025: Out of 37 pupils, 33 passed with flying colours, first position aggregate 306; 4 pupils were absent.

Co-Curricular & Extra-Curricular Activities

At Climax Imran Academy, we believe education is more than just academics. Our co-curricular and extra-curricular activities play a vital role in developing well-rounded students by fostering teamwork, creativity, leadership, and physical fitness.

Co-Curricular Activities

  • Debate Club: Developing critical thinking, public speaking, and persuasive skills.
  • Science and Math Club: Encouraging inquiry, experiments, and problem-solving beyond the syllabus.
  • Agriculture Club: Hands-on learning in sustainable farming and environmental stewardship.
  • Drama and Theatre: Building confidence, creativity, and expression through acting and stage production.
  • Music & Choir: Nurturing musical talents and promoting cultural appreciation.
  • ICT Club: Enhancing computer skills, coding, and digital literacy.
  • Religious & Moral Education Groups: Promoting values, ethics, and interfaith understanding.

Extra-Curricular Activities

  • Sports: Football, athletics, volleyball, and inter-house competitions.
  • Cultural Day: Annual event celebrating diversity through traditional music, dance, and food.
  • Clubs & Societies: Environmental club, art club, and community service groups.
  • Leadership & Student Council: Opportunities for students to develop leadership skills and represent their peers.
  • Field Trips & Educational Excursions: Hands-on learning experiences outside the classroom.
  • Health & Wellness Programs: Promoting physical and mental health awareness.
