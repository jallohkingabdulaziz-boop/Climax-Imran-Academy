Code of Conduct

(Guidelines for Behaviour, Discipline & Respect)

At Climax Imran Academy, we believe in creating a safe, respectful, and productive learning environment for all pupils. Every pupil is expected to conduct themselves in a manner that reflects honesty, discipline, hard work, and respect for others.

1️⃣ General Behaviour

  • Respect all staff, fellow pupils, and visitors at all times.
  • No offensive language, fighting, bullying, or harassment.
  • Remain within school premises during school hours unless given permission to leave.
  • No littering — use bins provided.
  • Cheating in tests or assignments is strictly prohibited.

2️⃣ Attendance & Punctuality

  • Pupils must attend school regularly and be punctual.
  • Absences must be explained by a written note from parents/guardians.
  • Persistent lateness or absenteeism will attract disciplinary action.

3️⃣ Uniform & Appearance

  • Wear the approved school uniform neatly and completely.
  • Jewellery (except small stud earrings for girls) is not allowed.
  • Hairstyles must be neat — no extreme styles or unnatural colours.
  • Shoes must be clean and in good condition.

4️⃣ Classroom Conduct

  • Listen attentively and participate in lessons.
  • No talking or disrupting during lessons.
  • Do homework and submit it on time.
  • Respect school property — no writing on desks or walls.

5️⃣ Respect for School Property

  • Care for textbooks, equipment, and facilities.
  • Damaging school property will require repair or replacement at the pupil’s expense.

6️⃣ Use of Electronic Devices

  • Mobile phones are not allowed during lessons without permission.
  • Misuse of devices (games, unauthorized photos, etc.) will result in confiscation.

7️⃣ Extra-Curricular Activities

  • Pupils are encouraged to join clubs, sports, and cultural events.
  • All activities must promote teamwork and sportsmanship.

8️⃣ Disciplinary Measures

Violations of the Code of Conduct may lead to:

  • Verbal warning
  • Written warning
  • Parent/guardian meeting
  • Suspension (temporary)
  • Expulsion (in extreme cases)

Clubs & Societies

(Building Skills, Talents & Team Spirit)

We encourage pupils to join at least one club or society to promote creativity, leadership, and teamwork.

1️⃣ Debating & Drama Club

Purpose: Improves public speaking, confidence, and critical thinking.
Activities: Debates, stage plays, poetry, and storytelling.

2️⃣ Science & Technology Club

Purpose: Develops interest in STEM.
Activities: Experiments, coding, robotics, quizzes.

3️⃣ Cultural & Heritage Society

Purpose: Preserves Sierra Leone’s traditions and values.
Activities: Cultural dances, fashion shows, folklore, language learning.

4️⃣ Music & Choir Group

Purpose: Builds musical skills and harmony.
Activities: Singing, instrument practice, school anthems, performances.

5️⃣ Sports & Fitness Club

Purpose: Promotes physical health, teamwork, and discipline.
Activities: Football, athletics, volleyball, games.

6️⃣ Red Cross / First Aid Society

Purpose: Teaches life-saving skills and volunteerism.
Activities: First aid training, safety patrol, outreach.

7️⃣ Environmental & Agriculture Club

Purpose: Encourages love for nature and eco-friendly habits.
Activities: Tree planting, recycling, gardening.

8️⃣ Press & Media Club

Purpose: Builds journalism and communication skills.
Activities: Newsletter, photography, interviews, event coverage.
