Admissions

Join Climax Imran Academy and become part of a vibrant learning community.

How to Apply

  1. Download and complete the application form.
  2. Submit the required documents (birth certificate, previous report cards).
  3. Pay admission fees at the school office.
  4. Attend orientation and receive class placement.
